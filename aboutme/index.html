About me

About me: I'm Milan Jankovic, a passionate photographer based in Switzerland. Photography has been my creative outlet for the past 4 years, allowing me to capture the beauty of landscapes, sunsets, and other mesmerizing moments that inspire me.



Growing up, I developed a deep appreciation for visual storytelling through photography. My journey began with an old phone's camera, and since then, I've been dedicated to honing my skills and exploring different techniques to convey emotions and narratives through my lens.



Beyond photography, I find joy in programming and video games. Sometimes I also play badminton. This diverse set of interests fuels my creativity and provides unique perspectives that influence my photographic style. My goal is always to evoke emotions and create lasting impressions through each photograph.
